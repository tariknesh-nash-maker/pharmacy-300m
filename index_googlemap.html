Pharmacy 300m — AI for Open Governance (Google Maps)

1) Identify your neighborhood

Use the search to go to an area (Tangier by default). Draw a polygon to focus recommendations.

2) Add existing pharmacies

Click the map to add a pharmacy, or import a CSV with columns: name,lat,lon. Each pharmacy shows a 300m exclusion circle.

3) Find recommendations

We propose points that are ≥300m from any existing pharmacy and close to other pharmacies (sign of demand).

4) Save for the future

You can import/export your work to continue later or share with others.
Why this app? Legal context & who benefits

Purpose. Turn the 300m pharmacy distance rule into a citizen-friendly map to help identify viable locations while respecting the law.

Legal context (Morocco). Pharmacies must be spaced at least 300 meters apart. This tool visualizes that rule to support transparent, evidence-based decisions.

Who benefits? Entrepreneurs planning a pharmacy, citizens who want better access to services, and public officials who value openness and data-driven planning.

Open data. When possible, use open, verifiable sources. You can also add known pharmacies manually or via CSV.
